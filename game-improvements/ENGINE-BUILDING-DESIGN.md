**Duration:** ~30 minutes  
**Core Mechanic:** Pure manual 3D platforming

**Gameplay:**
- You ARE the delivery person
- Run through 3D platformer levels
- Manually collect OKRs (golden cubes)
- Manually deliver to Jrod at level end

- 0-50 DP: Pure manual, learning the ropes

- 100-200 DP: Faster movement, easier platforming

### **üî• EVOLUTION 1: "The Team System" (First Reset)**

**What Happens:**
1. **You get promoted to "Manager"**

**Core Mechanic:** Platforming + Managing AI workforce

**The Dynamic Changes:**
- You still platformer through levels

- AI teammates run simpler paths autonomously
- You focus on complex routes and collecting "Management Points"

**Progression Milestones:**
